About Us

Get to know a little bit more about our company

We are a small company founded in 1973 and acquired in 1992 by the nowadays responsibles for the constant tradition. Having already gone through more than one reform, we always value the unforgettable experience lived by our customers, aiming for good service and always working with high quality products, a registered trademark of the bakery.


Open daily from 6am to 10pm, we offer a specialized service and a diversified menu, prepared by an extremely qualified team that is improving their skills every day.


Working with handmade and traditional recipes, we have a wide local recognition, where we keep a good communication and relationship with loyal and new customers, always treating them equally and with great care. One of our highlights is our fresh and tasty products, which pass through a rigorous quality control.
